---
title: Learner Profile
---

This course is aimed at learners who have basic comfort with creating
repeatable research scripts for publication, but are now interested
in making their code more re-usable outside the context of the original
research it was designed for, both by themselves but also with collaborators,
team members, and others.

It assumes some familiarity with Python, as we will be using that as the
language to demonstrate the process. Some familiarity with object-oriented
progrmming in Python may be useful in the last section of the course.
